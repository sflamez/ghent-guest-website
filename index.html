Discover Ghent

Cultural Highlights

Ghent is a city teeming with history and art. Don't miss the Gravensteen, a 10th-century castle with stunning city views. Marvel at the Adoration of the Mystic Lamb, the world-renowned altarpiece by the Van Eyck brothers in St. Bavo's Cathedral. For a modern twist, explore the street art that colors the city's alleys.

Culinary Delights

Ghent is a paradise for food lovers. Be sure to try "Gentse neuzen" (Ghent noses), traditional cone-shaped candies. For a hearty meal, indulge in "stoverij," a rich Flemish beef and beer stew. The city is also known as the vegetarian capital of Europe, so you'll find plenty of delicious plant-based options.

Shopping Guide

From high-end brands on the Veldstraat to unique boutiques in the smaller streets, Ghent offers a diverse shopping experience. Explore the Patershol neighborhood for artisan shops and galleries. On Sundays, enjoy the lively atmosphere of the Kouter flower market.
